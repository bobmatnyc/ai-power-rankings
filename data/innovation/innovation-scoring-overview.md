# AI Power Ranking - Innovation Scoring Overview

## What is Innovation Scoring?

Innovation scoring represents 15% of our total ranking algorithm and evaluates breakthrough capabilities, paradigm shifts, and technological advancement in AI coding tools. Unlike other metrics that measure current performance, innovation scoring specifically identifies tools that are pushing the boundaries of what's possible in AI-assisted development.

## Scoring Scale (0-10)

- **9-10**: Revolutionary breakthrough that fundamentally changes development
- **7-8**: Major innovation with significant industry impact
- **5-6**: Notable advancement beyond current standards
- **3-4**: Incremental improvement over existing solutions
- **1-2**: Minimal innovation, mostly following established patterns
- **0**: No meaningful innovation

## How We Track Innovation

Innovation scores are evaluated monthly based on:

- Feature releases and technical announcements
- Benchmark performance on novel tasks
- Developer community adoption of new paradigms
- Industry impact and competitive responses
- Academic papers and technical documentation

## Why Innovation Matters

While market traction and technical performance are important, innovation scoring helps identify:

- Tools that will define the future of development
- Early movers in emerging categories
- Potential disruption before it shows in adoption metrics
- Long-term leaders vs. short-term winners

Innovation scores naturally decay over time as breakthrough features become table stakes, ensuring our rankings always highlight the cutting edge of AI coding technology.
